   1  Welcome to Yasyf's Cousin Garvey's Den!
   2  This is a simple turn-based game developed completely using AS 2 and PHP. 
   3  The objective of the game is either to drop cages on an enemy player, trapping him, or to avoid the cages being dropped.
   4  The way this is accomplished is that each game starts with three cages positioned across the center of a 5x3 grid at positions (1,2), (3,2), and (5,2), and a player at (3,2).
   5  On Player 1's turn, he can move his player one square in any 90° direction (up, down, left, right), space permitting
   6  On Player 2's turn, he can move one of his three cages to anywhere on the grid (besides from the location occupied by the other two)
   7  Player 2 will move first.
   8  After both player's have made a move, the turn number increments, and the cages 'fall' one increment towards the ground.
   9  After 8 increments (8 moves EACH), Player 2 will not make a move on the 9th turn, but observe.
  10  Player 1 will make his 9th (final) turn, and then the game will decide the winner.
  11  If the player is trapped, Player 2 wins. If the player escapes, Player 1 wins.
  12  All communication between the two players is done via a PHP API and its connected MySQL Database.
  13  All in-game data is proccessed independently on either end by AS 2, based on what the API returns.
