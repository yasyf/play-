   1  # Welcome to [Yasyf's Cousin Garvey's Den](http://yasyf.github.com/play-/)!
   2  
   3  [Visit Homepage To Play](http://garveysden.yasyf.com)
   4  
   5  
   6  This is a simple turn-based game developed completely using AS 2 and PHP. 
   7  
   8  The objective of the game is either to drop cages on an enemy player, trapping him, or to avoid the cages being dropped.
   9  
  10  The way this is accomplished is that each game starts with three cages positioned across the center of a 5x3 grid at positions (1,2), (3,2), and (5,2), and a player at (3,2).
  11  
  12  On Player 1's turn, he can move his player one square in any 90° direction (up, down, left, right), space permitting
  13  
  14  On Player 2's turn, he can move one of his three cages to anywhere on the grid (besides from the location occupied by the other two)
  15  
  16  Player 2 will move first.
  17  
  18  After both player's have made a move, the turn number increments, and the cages 'fall' one increment towards the ground.
  19  
  20  After 7 increments (7 moves EACH), Player 2 will not make a move on the 8th turn, but observe.
  21  
  22  Player 1 will make his 8th (final) turn, and then the game will decide the winner.
  23  
  24  If the player is trapped, Player 2 wins. If the player escapes, Player 1 wins.
  25  
  26  All communication between the two players is done via a PHP API and its connected MySQL Database.
  27  
  28  All in-game data is processed independently on either end by AS 2, based on what the API returns.
