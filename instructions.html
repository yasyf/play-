Welcome to Yasyf's Cousin Garvey's Den!

Instructions:

Player 1:
  1. Use the arrow keys to move your player around
  2. You have nine turns to move your player (one direction,one square per turn)
  3. Your opponent will be rearanging the falling blocks above your head, moving one to any location per turn
  4. The blocks will fall closer one increment each turn
  5. After the ninth turn, if you are not hit by a landing block, you win!
Player 2:
  1. Use your mouse to drag the block around
  2. You have nine turns to move your block (one block per turn)
  3. Your opponent will be moving his player (one direction,one square per turn), trying to avoid your blocks
  4. The blocks will fall down one increment each turn
  5. After the ninth turn, if you hit the player with a landing block, you win!
